About

Quizzical is a general knowledge quiz game where users can select from a variety of options to create a customized game experience.

To play quizzical simply hit start, select your options and enter your name! Once the game concludes you will be scored and hopefully earn a place in the hall of fame!

This game utilizes assets made by myself as well as the OpenTriviaDB api for our questions!
